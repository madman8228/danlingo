## sentencePatterns: It returns that + 从句
- example: After analyzing the experimental data, it returns that the new drug has a 90% success rate in treating the disease. || 在分析完实验数据后，结果显示这种新药治疗该疾病的成功率达到90%。
- example: When the algorithm finishes running, it returns that the best route to the destination is through the coastal highway. || 算法运行结束后，结果显示去往目的地的最佳路线是走滨海公路。

## sentencePatterns: It takes sb + 时间 + to do sth
- example: It takes me about 45 minutes to commute from my apartment to the office every weekday. || 每个工作日我从公寓通勤到办公室需要大约45分钟。
- example: It took the construction team two whole years to complete this landmark skyscraper in the city center. || 这支施工队花了整整两年时间才建成了市中心的这栋地标性摩天大楼。

## sentencePatterns: be fetched from sp
- example: The rare ancient books displayed in the museum are fetched from the underground storage room every morning by professional staff. || 博物馆里展出的珍贵古籍每天早上都会由专业工作人员从地下储藏室取出来。
- example: The fresh seafood served in this restaurant is fetched from the nearby fishing port before dawn every day. || 这家餐厅供应的新鲜海鲜每天天亮前都会从附近的渔港运过来。

## sentencePatterns: be seized with sth
- example: When she stood on the stage to give a speech for the first time, she was seized with a sudden panic and forgot all her lines. || 她第一次站在台上演讲时，突然一阵恐慌，把台词全都忘了。
- example: Many old people in the village were seized with a high fever after the sudden cold wave hit the area. || 寒潮突然袭击这个地区后，村里的很多老人都发起了高烧。

## sentencePatterns: fetch sb sth
- example: It's raining heavily outside, could you fetch me an umbrella from the storage closet before I go out? || 外面雨下得很大，我出门前你能帮我从储藏柜里拿一把伞吗？
- example: When grandma comes to visit this weekend, I will fetch her her favorite jasmine tea from the tea cabinet as soon as she enters the door. || 奶奶周末来做客的时候，我会在她进门的第一时间就从茶柜里给她拿她最喜欢的茉莉花茶。

## sentencePatterns: fetch sth from sp
- example: Please fetch my glasses from the bedside table before I go to read. || 我去看书之前，请帮我从床头柜把我的眼镜拿过来。
- example: The little boy volunteered to fetch some fresh water from the well for the elderly neighbor. || 小男孩主动提出要帮年迈的邻居从井里打一些淡水回来。

## sentencePatterns: give sb sth
- example: My grandma always gives me homemade snacks every time I visit her on weekends. || 我每次周末去看望奶奶的时候，她总会给我一些自制的小吃。
- example: The kind teacher gave the poor student a new schoolbag and a full set of stationery at the beginning of the semester. || 那位善良的老师在学期初给了这个贫困学生一个新书包和一整套文具。

## sentencePatterns: give sth to sb
- example: Remember to give the signed contract to the manager as soon as you arrive at the office tomorrow. || 你明天到办公室之后，记得把签好的合同交给经理。
- example: The little girl gave the handmade birthday card to her mother with a bright smile on her face. || 小女孩脸上带着灿烂的笑容，把亲手做的生日贺卡送给了妈妈。

## sentencePatterns: give up doing sth
- example: Even though he encountered many difficulties during the experiment, he never gave up trying to find a better solution. || 尽管他在实验过程中遇到了很多困难，他从未放弃寻找更好解决方案的尝试。
- example: The doctor advised my father to give up smoking and drinking to improve his heart health. || 医生建议我父亲戒烟戒酒，以此改善他的心脏健康状况。

## sentencePatterns: grab + sth + to do
- example: I usually grab a bottle of milk and a sandwich to eat on my way to work when I get up late. || 我起晚的时候，通常会拿一瓶牛奶和一个三明治，在去上班的路上吃。
- example: We need to grab some camping tents and sleeping bags to use during our trip to the mountain this weekend. || 我们需要拿一些露营帐篷和睡袋，供这周末进山旅行的时候使用。

## sentencePatterns: grab sb + by + 身体部位
- example: The police officer grabbed the thief by the arm to stop him from running away. || 警察抓住小偷的胳膊，阻止他逃跑。
- example: In a panic, she grabbed her little brother by the collar to pull him back from the edge of the cliff. || 慌乱中，她抓住弟弟的衣领，把他从悬崖边拉了回来。

## sentencePatterns: it is time to grab + sth
- example: It is time to grab your backpack and head to the railway station, or you will miss the train. || 是时候拿上你的背包去火车站了，不然你会错过火车的。
- example: The bell for the end of class just rang, and it is time to grab a quick bite at the cafeteria before the afternoon lecture starts. || 下课铃刚响，是时候在下午讲座开始前去食堂快速吃点东西了。

## sentencePatterns: return sth to sb
- example: Please remember to return the reference book to the librarian before you leave the library. || 请记得在离开图书馆之前把这本参考书还给图书管理员。
- example: I will return the umbrella you lent me to you tomorrow when we meet at the office. || 明天我们在办公室见面的时候，我会把你借给我的伞还给你。

## sentencePatterns: return to + 地点/状态
- example: After spending three years studying abroad, she finally returned to her hometown last month. || 在国外留学三年后，她上个月终于回到了自己的家乡。
- example: With the doctor's professional treatment and careful nursing, the patient soon returned to normal health. || 在医生的专业治疗和精心护理下，这位患者很快就恢复了健康状态。

## sentencePatterns: seize sth from sb
- example: The brave security guard seized the sharp knife from the attacker before he could hurt anyone else. || 勇敢的保安在袭击者伤到其他人之前，从他手里夺下了锋利的刀。
- example: The customs officers seized a large quantity of smuggled cigarettes from the illegal trader at the border checkpoint. || 海关人员在边境检查站从非法商贩手里缴获了大量走私香烟。

## sentencePatterns: seize the chance to do sth
- example: You should seize the chance to study abroad when the opportunity presents itself. || 当机会来临时，你应该抓住出国留学的机会。
- example: Many small businesses seized the chance to expand their online sales during the e-commerce boom. || 电商繁荣时期，很多小企业抓住了拓展线上销售业务的机会。

## sentencePatterns: take + 宾语 + 宾语补足语(adj./n.)
- example: The teacher took the student's creative idea seriously and encouraged him to develop it further. || 老师很重视这名学生的创意想法，鼓励他进一步深化这个想法。
- example: We all take him a reliable partner because he always keeps his promises in every cooperation. || 我们都把他看作可靠的合作伙伴，因为他在每一次合作中都信守承诺。

## sentencePatterns: take it + adj. + that 从句
- example: You can take it for granted that the local guide will arrange all the accommodation and transportation during the trip. || 你可以放心，当地导游会安排好旅行期间所有的食宿和交通。
- example: The manager took it certain that the new product would capture a large share of the market once it was launched. || 经理确信这款新产品一经推出就会占据很大的市场份额。
