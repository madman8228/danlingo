## sentencePatterns: It is adopted that + clause
- example: It is adopted that the new environmental policy will take effect starting next month. || 新的环境政策将从下月起生效，这一方案已被采纳。
- example: It is adopted that the annual meeting will be held online to avoid unnecessary gatherings. || 年会将在线上举办以避免不必要聚集的安排已被通过。

## sentencePatterns: It is agreed that...
- example: It is agreed that we need to set aside 10% of the budget for emergency use. || 我们一致同意需要留出10%的预算作为应急资金。
- example: It is agreed that the team will submit the progress report every Friday before work. || 大家达成共识，团队每周五下班前都要提交进度报告。

## sentencePatterns: accept + noun/pronoun
- example: The university has accepted his application for the postgraduate program. || 这所大学已经通过了他的研究生项目申请。
- example: She didn't accept the expensive gift from her colleague to avoid misunderstanding. || 她没有收下同事送的昂贵礼物，以免产生误会。

## sentencePatterns: accept sb/sth as + noun/adjective
- example: The global community generally accepts this research conclusion as valid and reliable. || 国际社会普遍认为这份研究结论是有效且可靠的。
- example: After years of hard work, he was finally accepted as a formal member of the professional association. || 经过多年的努力，他终于被接纳为该专业协会的正式成员。

## sentencePatterns: accept that-clause
- example: You have to accept that not every effort you make will bring you the expected reward. || 你必须接受一点：不是你付出的每一份努力都能带来预期的回报。
- example: The company has accepted that the original marketing strategy is not suitable for the current market environment. || 该公司已经承认，原本的营销策略并不适用于当前的市场环境。

## sentencePatterns: adopt + noun
- example: The city council decided to adopt a new environmental policy to reduce carbon emissions. || 市议会决定通过一项新的环保政策来减少碳排放。
- example: Many young couples choose to adopt a stray pet to give them a warm home. || 很多年轻情侣选择收养一只流浪宠物，给它们一个温暖的家。

## sentencePatterns: adopt sb as + 身份名词
- example: The local community decided to adopt the kind volunteer as their honorary resident. || 当地社区决定将那位善良的志愿者吸纳为他们的荣誉居民。
- example: The famous artist agreed to adopt the talented teenager as his only apprentice. || 那位著名艺术家同意收这个有天赋的少年作为他唯一的徒弟。

## sentencePatterns: agree to do sth
- example: My parents finally agreed to let me go on a graduation trip with my classmates. || 我父母最终同意让我和同学们一起去毕业旅行。
- example: The company has agreed to provide free training courses for all new employees. || 该公司已经同意为所有新员工提供免费的培训课程。

## sentencePatterns: agree with sb about/on sth
- example: I agree with my supervisor about the adjustment direction of the project schedule. || 我同意我主管关于项目进度调整方向的看法。
- example: Most members of the team agree with the team leader on the priority of the upcoming tasks. || 团队的大部分成员都认同 leader 关于后续任务优先级的安排。

## sentencePatterns: be received well/badly
- example: The newly released fantasy film was received well by audiences of all age groups. || 这部新上映的奇幻电影受到了各个年龄段观众的好评。
- example: The new pricing policy launched by the platform was received badly by the majority of users. || 该平台推出的新定价政策遭到了大部分用户的负面评价。

## sentencePatterns: be rejected by + sb/sth
- example: His application for the master's program was rejected by the top university last month. || 他申请的硕士项目上个月被那所顶尖大学拒绝了。
- example: She felt heartbroken when she was rejected by the boy she had liked for three years. || 当她被喜欢了三年的男孩拒绝时，她感到非常伤心。

## sentencePatterns: receive sth as sth
- example: The strange custom is received as a symbol of good luck by the local villagers. || 这个奇特的习俗被当地村民视作好运的象征。
- example: His constructive suggestions were received as valuable guidance by the entire project team. || 他提出的建设性建议被整个项目团队当作宝贵的指导。

## sentencePatterns: receive sth from sb
- example: I received a delicate handwritten birthday card from my pen pal who lives in Canada last week. || 上周我收到了住在加拿大的笔友寄来的一张精美的手写生日贺卡。
- example: The disaster-stricken area received tons of emergency relief supplies from warm-hearted people across the country. || 灾区收到了来自全国各地热心民众捐赠的数吨紧急救援物资。

## sentencePatterns: refuse + sb + sth
- example: The kind waiter refused the elderly customer the extra payment for the spilled soup. || 好心的服务员拒绝了老年顾客为洒掉的汤额外支付的费用。
- example: The local government refuses low-income families the right to apply for public rental housing without proper reason. || 当地政府不会无理由拒绝低收入家庭申请公租房的权利。

## sentencePatterns: refuse + to-infinitive
- example: The little boy refused to eat any vegetables even though his parents kept persuading him. || 尽管父母一直劝说，这个小男孩还是拒绝吃任何蔬菜。
- example: The responsible engineer refused to sign the completion report before all potential safety hazards were eliminated. || 这位负责任的工程师在所有安全隐患被消除之前拒绝签署竣工报告。

## sentencePatterns: reject + sb/sth + as + adj/n
- example: The company rejected his application as unsuitable for the position. || 公司驳回了他的申请，认为他不适合这个岗位。
- example: Many critics rejected the new policy as ineffective in solving the housing crisis. || 许多批评者抵制这项新政策，认为它在解决住房危机方面毫无作用。
- example: She was rejected by the research team as unqualified for the core experiment task. || 她被研究团队拒之门外，被认为没有资格承担核心实验任务。

## sentencePatterns: reject + sth
- example: The university rejected his admission request because his grades did not meet the minimum requirement. || 这所大学驳回了他的入学申请，因为他的成绩没有达到最低要求。
- example: Local residents rejected the construction plan of the chemical plant over concerns about environmental pollution. || 当地居民反对建设化工厂的计划，原因是担忧环境污染问题。
- example: You can reject the unreasonable terms in the contract before signing it. || 在签署合同之前，你可以拒绝接受合同里的不合理条款。
