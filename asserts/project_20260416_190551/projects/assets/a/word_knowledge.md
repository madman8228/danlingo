- us: /rɪˈdʒekt/
- synonyms: refuse || 拒绝 ;; decline || 谢绝 ;; turn down || 驳回 ;; repudiate || 否定 ;; discard || 丢弃 ;;
- antonyms: accept || 接受 ;; approve || 批准 ;; adopt || 采纳 ;;
- phrases: reject a proposal || 拒绝提案 ;; reject an application || 驳回申请 ;; reject out of hand || 断然拒绝 ;; reject faulty goods || 退回次品 ;;
- collocations: officially reject || 正式驳回 ;; flatly reject || 断然拒绝 ;; reluctantly reject || 不情愿地拒绝 ;; reject an argument || 驳斥论点 ;; reject a request || 拒绝请求 ;;
- sentencePatterns: reject + sth || 后接名词/代词作宾语，表示拒绝某事物 ;; reject + sb/sth + as + adj/n || 表示认为某人/某物是某种情况而拒绝 ;; be rejected by + sb/sth || 被动语态，表示被某人/某机构拒绝 ;;
- spokenExpressions: I had to reject it. || 我只能拒绝它。 ;; My application got rejected. || 我的申请被拒了。 ;; Don't reject the idea out of hand. || 别一下子就否定这个想法。 ;;
- idioms: reject the null hypothesis || 否定零假设（统计学术语，引申为推翻原有假设） ;;

### sense: 动词（v.）
- translationZh: 拒绝 ;; 驳回 ;; 拒收 ;; 不录用 ;; 排斥 ;;
- definitionZh: 拒绝接受或同意某事物 ;; 不认可、不通过申请、提案等 ;; 因质量问题退回物品 ;; 不录用求职者、不录取申请者 ;; 生物机体对移植器官等产生排异反应 ;;
- definitionEn: to refuse to accept, believe in, or agree with something ;; to officially refuse to allow a request, application, or plan to succeed ;; to send back goods that are not good enough to be sold or used ;; to decide not to hire someone or accept them for a course of study ;; if a person's body rejects a transplanted organ, it reacts to the organ as if it is harmful and tries to destroy it ;;
- example: The committee unanimously rejected the proposal to build a new shopping mall in the residential area. || 委员会一致否决了在居民区新建购物中心的提案。
- example: My visa application was rejected because I didn't provide sufficient proof of financial support. || 我的签证申请被驳回了，因为我没有提供足够的资金证明。
- example: Patients who receive organ transplants need to take special medicine so that their bodies do not reject the new organ. || 接受器官移植的病人需要服用特殊药物，避免身体对新器官产生排异反应。

### sense: 名词（n.）
- translationZh: 被拒绝的人 ;; 次品 ;; 不合格产品 ;;
- definitionZh: 未被选中、未被录取的人 ;; 因质量不合格被淘汰的产品 ;;
- definitionEn: a person who has been rejected by a particular group, organization, or society ;; a product that cannot be sold or used because it has something wrong with it ;;
- example: He felt like a social reject after none of the universities he applied to sent him an offer. || 在申请的大学都没有发来录取通知书后，他觉得自己是个被社会排斥的人。
- example: These rejects from the factory are sold at a discounted price in the outlet store. || 这些工厂的不合格次品在折扣店里低价出售。
- example: The quality inspection team threw all the manufacturing rejects into the recycling bin. || 质检团队把所有生产残次品都扔进了回收箱。
