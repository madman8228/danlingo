- sentencePatterns: sb/committee + reject a proposal + on the grounds that + clause || 某人/委员会以某理由否决一项提案，说明否决的依据 ;; sb + reject a proposal + put forward by + proposer || 某人驳回由某提出者提交的提议，说明提案的发起方 ;;
- spokenExpressions: The board will definitely reject that proposal, it's way too risky. || 董事会肯定会否决那个提案，风险太大了。 ;; I can't believe they rejected our proposal after all the work we put in. || 我们费了那么大功夫，他们居然把我们的提案驳回来了，真是不敢信。 ;;
- idioms: reject a proposal out of hand || 不假思索地驳回提案 ;;
- example: The city council rejected the proposal to build a new factory near the residential area. || 市议会否决了在居民区附近新建工厂的提案。
- example: He was very upset when the manager rejected his proposal for optimizing the work process. || 经理驳回了他优化工作流程的提议，他感到非常沮丧。

## phrases: reject an application
- collocations: automatically reject an application || 自动驳回一份申请 ;; arbitrarily reject an application || 随意驳回一份申请 ;; officially reject an application || 正式拒绝一份申请 ;;
- sentencePatterns: institution + reject an application + due to + lack of sth || 机构因缺少某材料拒绝某申请，说明拒签/拒申请的原因 ;; sb + be notified that + their application + be rejected || 某人收到通知其申请被驳回，说明告知的语境 ;;
- spokenExpressions: My visa application got rejected again, what a bummer. || 我的签证申请又被拒了，真倒霉。 ;; The university rejected my application, so I'm going to apply to other schools. || 那所大学拒了我的申请，所以我打算申请别的学校。 ;;

- example: The immigration office rejected his work visa application because he didn't provide enough proof of employment. || 移民局驳回了他的工作签证申请，因为他没有提供足够的就业证明。
- example: If you don't fill in all the required fields, the system will reject your application immediately. || 如果你没有填完所有必填项，系统会立刻驳回你的申请。

## phrases: reject faulty goods
- collocations: reject faulty goods immediately || 立即拒收瑕疵货物 ;; reject faulty goods according to regulations || 按规定拒收瑕疵货物 ;; reject faulty goods from suppliers || 拒收供应商提供的瑕疵货物 ;;
- sentencePatterns: sb. has the right to reject faulty goods when + condition || 当满足某条件时，某人有权拒收瑕疵货物 ;; Businesses are required to reject faulty goods to + purpose || 企业需要拒收瑕疵货物以达成某目的 ;;
- spokenExpressions: I'm sorry, but we have to reject these faulty goods. || 不好意思，我们只能拒收这些有问题的货物。 ;; Can I reject these faulty goods and get a full refund? || 我能不能拒收这些瑕疵货，然后拿全额退款？ ;;
- idioms:
- example: Customers are entitled to reject faulty goods within 14 days of receiving their order. || 顾客有权在收货后的14天内拒收瑕疵货物。
- example: The supermarket will reject faulty goods delivered by the manufacturer to protect consumer interests. || 这家超市会拒收厂商送来的瑕疵货物，以此保护消费者的权益。

## phrases: reject out of hand
- collocations: reject the proposal out of hand || 直接拒绝该提议 ;; reject the request out of hand || 断然拒绝该请求 ;; reject the accusation out of hand || 当即驳斥该指控 ;;
- sentencePatterns: sb. reject sth. out of hand without + reason/action || 某人没有做某件事就直接拒绝了某事物 ;; The idea/suggestion is rejected out of hand by + party || 某个想法/建议被某方当即拒绝 ;;
- spokenExpressions: He just rejected my suggestion out of hand, not even giving me a chance to explain. || 他直接就拒绝了我的建议，连给我解释的机会都没有。 ;; Don't reject the plan out of hand—maybe we can adjust some parts to make it work. || 别一口否决这个计划啊，说不定我们可以调整部分内容让它可行。 ;;
- idioms:
- example: When the manager heard the budget would be 50% higher than expected, he rejected the plan out of hand. || 经理一听到预算会比预期高50%，当即就否决了这个方案。
- example: The company rejected the rumors of a potential merger out of hand during the press conference. || 该公司在发布会上直接驳斥了可能并购的谣言。
