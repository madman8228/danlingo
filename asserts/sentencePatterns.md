## pattern: It is miserable for sb to do sth
- 中文: For someone, doing something is miserable and hard to bear.
- 结构: It + is + miserable + for sb + to do sth.
- 用法: Use this pattern when describing strong discomfort or emotional pain caused by an action.
- 例句1: It is miserable for him to work in the noise all day. || 对他来说，整天在噪音中工作很痛苦。
- 例句2: It is miserable for her to relive that memory again. || 对她来说，再次回想那段记忆很难受。
- 相关句型: It is painful for sb to do sth || It is unbearable for sb to do sth

## pattern: It is painful for sb to do sth
- 中文: For someone, doing something is painful.
- 结构: It + is + painful + for sb + to do sth.
- 用法: Use this pattern for physical pain or emotional pain triggered by an action.
- 例句1: It is painful for me to stand for too long. || 对我来说，站太久会很痛。
- 例句2: It is painful for her to talk about that loss. || 对她来说，谈起那次失去很痛苦。
- 相关句型: It is miserable for sb to do sth || It is unbearable for sb to do sth

## pattern: It is unbearable for sb to do sth
- 中文: For someone, doing something is unbearable.
- 结构: It + is + unbearable + for sb + to do sth.
- 用法: Use this pattern when the discomfort exceeds one's tolerance.
- 例句1: It is unbearable for me to sit here without air conditioning. || 对我来说，没有空调坐在这里无法忍受。
- 例句2: It is unbearable for him to hear that accusation repeatedly. || 对他来说，反复听到那种指责无法忍受。
- 相关句型: It is painful for sb to do sth || It is miserable for sb to do sth
